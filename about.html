What is Re2pect?

Re2Pect is literally your worth in the LHS Percussion Section. If you are on the Re2pect board, congratulations! You have made it to the top, or bottom, of worth in the LHS Percussion section! That means the admins find you either really worth something, or want you to radically change your belief systems, behaviors, and actions, as to please them. If you aren't on the board, you are currently average, which is bad. That means you can easily slip to the bottom of the Re2pect chart, and your life will get significantly worse.

Bottom line: Re2pect is the most important aspect of your life.


Gaining Re2pect

You can gain Re2pect through the following ways:

  • Doing something cool
  • Performing a 1 minute uke solo
  • Working at McDanks
  • saying something Uber funny
  • Add something worthwhile to the percussion room (see below)
  • Win game of Fortnite, PUBG, or Rules of Survival (provide proof)
  • Win a game of Bang Boi Ball
  • Score a 3 in Bang Boi Ball

As you can see, there are lots of ways to get started earning Re2pect!


Losing Re2pect

While there are many ways to earn your way up to the top, there are also ways to hurt your Re2pect score... these include:

  • Having Brain Cancer
  • Having a brother who is better than you
  • Saying something stupid
  • Talking back to Re2pect admins
  • Having a low Re2pect score
  • Missing a 3 in bang boi ball

Make sure you're careful not to do any of these things, and you will be fine!
